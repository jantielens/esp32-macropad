Label Style Reference

Override individual label styling with a compact key:value;... syntax.

font Font size: 12, 14, 18, 24, 32, or 36
align left, center, or right
y Vertical pixel offset (e.g. -2, 5)
mode clip, scroll, dot (...), or wrap
color Hex color (e.g. #FF0, #FF6600)

Examples

font:36;align:left;mode:dot Large, left-aligned, ellipsis
font:14;color:#FF0 Small yellow text
y:-4;align:right Shifted up, right-aligned
